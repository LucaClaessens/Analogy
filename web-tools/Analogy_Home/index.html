We believe publishers shouldn’t
be left out of business by industry leaders that have highly profitable production models.

Analogy is a consultancy specialized in adapting the advances of innovative online platforms (Google, Facebook, Spotify etc.) to update traditional publishing practices. By constantly analyzing successful models, Analogy offers solutions that bring agility to the traditional publishing workflow.

or each project, Analogy combines an array of strategies employed by industry leaders (neural networks, crowdsourcing, freemium etc.) to develop a new publishing model. Our models work to maximise efficiency and optimise the expenditure of each phase of the publishing process.

Our aim is simple: to adopt the strategy of the winner.

Recently we've been working with
MoneyLab #3: Failing better.
We have developed a three part workflow to enhance their practice:

Recombination

Applying the technique used by Google Books, the Neural Network is trained using the MoneyLab’s text-based resources (blog posts and published essays) to generate a constant flow of content. The Neural Network structures this content into a series of sentences that relate to the MoneyLab’s broader research objectives. By using the existing dataset, the Neural Network optimizes the MoneyLab’s publishing workflow by offsetting the human costs (time, money and effort) associated with content generation.

Valuation

Using the logic of Facebook’s ranking algorithm and Amazon Kindle’s popular highlights, the editing interface allows users to edit the dataset produced by the Neural Network on behalf of MoneyLab. In this way the editing process is outsourced. By tracking inputs, users are employed to separate the popular content from the unpopular. The former is graded as premium, while the latter is discarded. Users can also take part in an image-based selection process, categorizing the images according to four key words, in order to link the images back to the premium content.

Dissemination

Adopting the principles of Freemium, Analogy offers a publication divided into two versions: free and premium. The free publication contains the unpopular content as determined by the user selection process. The free version includes advertising, unedited content, but contains no images. The premium publication contains popular content and tagged images. The premium version is ad free and is distributed in a custom designed Analogy folder.
