Leave the production of your publications to the fastest, most cutting edge company.

Producing a publication comes with a multitude of costs. The traditional publishing workflow - gathering content, editing, copy editing, design and printing - is time consuming and resource heavy. What is needed is a new publishing model developed to work with with a level of efficiency that corresponds to the pace at which content is generated and consumed today. Analogy is designed to address this need.

Recombination.

Applying techniques used by Google Books, our Neural Network is trained using text-based resources to generate a constant flow of content. By using the existing dataset, the Neural Network optimizes the client's publishing workflow by offsetting the human costs (time, money and effort) associated with content generation.

Dynamic content production

We deploy a trained neural network that converts your content into published text. With access to your unique data set, we adapt our neural network to meet your publishing requirements.

Automated content editing

We have a bunch of robots automatically writing text for you, they get trained by our specialists to write according to your needs and will compile this writing accordingly.

Outsourced design

In most cases, the editorial work will be divided into many small tasks and handed over to an army of microlabourers, ensuring high quality production for a small fee.

Information Access

we aggregate information based on the needs of your request, preferrably we'd like to have access to parts of your data on the topic.

Ad-sponsored printing

We work with different production models, but the most common is ad-sponsored printing. The introduction of sponsored content either as advertisement or product placement within the source text passed through the system.
